One of the most popular TV game shows where you try to find $1,000,000 among 26 cases! No particular aptitude, lateral thinking or stunts involved - just an element of luck, nerves of steel and a sense of timing as you battle against the odds and the banker who is, as always, up to his devious tricks! Will you be able to win a fortune or at least make a good deal? Or will you yield to the bankerâ€™s manipulative ways? Or will you end up with almost nothing?
  1. To begin, you choose a case for yourself. To determine what amount is inside your chosen case, you pick cases from the remaining pool of 25 to be opened. The amounts in these cases are then revealed and removed from the board, indicating that they cannot be in your case by elimination.
  2. Each time the banker calls, you have 15 seconds to decide between Deal and No Deal:
    • Deal to accept his offer in exchange for your case without knowing what's inside;
    • No Deal to decline his offer and continue picking cases to be opened.
  3. The banker calls in for the last time when there are two cases left - namely your case and the only unopened case standing. If you choose No Deal, you will have to decide between keeping your original case and swapping with the other! Your winnings will be the amount in the case eventually taken!
